Daylight Rooftop Headshot Sessions (NYC)

Full Update

The daylight headshot session is meticulously crafted to provide each model and talent with a comprehensive and polished update to their headshots. Perfect for refreshing an existing portfolio or starting one anew, these sessions are designed to capture the best of you in the natural light of a stunning rooftop setting.

Session Details:

  • Duration: 1 - 1.5 hours
  • Look Changes: 2
  • Edited Images: 14 - 20 fully edited images
  • Delivery Time: Within 7 days of the shoot date

These sessions are an excellent opportunity for anyone in need of professional, high-quality headshots to elevate their portfolio. Whether you are a seasoned model, an aspiring actor, or someone simply looking to update your professional image, this session is tailored to meet your needs.
